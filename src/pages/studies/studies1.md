Broadening my expertise in the intersection of mathematics, statistics, and computer science to derive actionable insights from complex data. This intensive program at the University of Colombo focuses on:

- **Mathematical Foundations**: Advanced study in Pure and Applied Mathematics, providing the rigorous basis for statistical algorithms and data analysis.
- **Statistical Modelling**: Mastery of probabilistic models, regression analysis, and predictive analytics to solve real-world problems.
- **Machine Learning & AI**: Hands-on experience with supervised and unsupervised learning, neural networks, and deep learning architectures.
- **Data Analytics & Visualization**: Developing skills in interpreting large datasets and communicating findings through professional-grade visualization tools.
- **Computer Science Integration**: Applying data structures, algorithms, and software engineering principles to optimize data processing pipelines.
