TS Technologies is a leading digital solutions provider in Sri Lanka, specializing in systems integration and smart automation for sectors like banking, healthcare, and retail. During my internship, I contributed to their suite of customer experience and operational efficiency tools, including:

- **Queue Management Systems**: Developed and refined interactive interfaces for both physical and virtual queuing solutions, streamlining customer flow for major banking and healthcare clients.
- **Digital Signage & Feedback Solutions**: Assisted in building dynamic content management systems for digital signage and real-time customer feedback tools.
- **Interactive Smart Panels**: Worked on UI components for advanced collaboration panels used in corporate and educational environments.

### Key Responsibilities & Learning:
- **Frontend Development (React)**: Focused on building modular, reusable components for enterprise-level web applications. Refactored legacy CSS into utility-first patterns using Tailwind CSS to improve maintainability and performance.
- **Backend Development (Java Spring Boot)**: Gained experience in developing RESTful APIs and integrating MySQL/MongoDB databases for data-driven applications.
- **Enterprise Integration**: Assisted in the digital transformation of business operations by implementing features for people counting and automated reporting systems.
- **Full-Stack Collaboration**: Participated in the complete software development lifecycle (SDLC), from front-end component design to back-end API connectivity.
